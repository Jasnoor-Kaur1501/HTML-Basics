Why Kettles Matter

Kettles control flow, temperature stability, and turbulence.

Flow rate changes how quickly compounds dissolve. Spout shape changes where energy enters the coffee bed.

The kettle is not a container. It is a precision instrument.

Brewers Create Environments

Brewers do not make coffee. They create chemical conditions.

Filters remove oils. Pressure traps gases. Immersion equalizes concentration.

The brewer decides what chemistry is even possible.

The Equipment Hierarchy

Not all tools matter equally.

Grinder first. Water second. Brewer third. Everything else last.

Brew quality scales with particle control.
