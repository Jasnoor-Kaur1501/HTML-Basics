Methods Are Not Recipes

Brew methods are not instructions. They are systems.

Every method is a way of controlling how water meets coffee. Pressure. Flow. Contact time. Turbulence. Filtration.

Espresso, pour-over, cold brew — these are not drinks. They are extraction environments.
